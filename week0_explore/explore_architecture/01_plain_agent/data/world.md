# World Map

## Midgaard

### Navigation Route to Bakery (from spawn)
1. `down` — Reception → Entrance Hall of the Grunting Boar Inn
2. `west` — Entrance Hall → Temple Square
3. `north` — Temple Square → Main Street (west end)
4. `east` — Main Street (middle)
5. `north` — Main Street → **The Bakery**

### Bakery
- **Location:** North of Main Street (between West Gate and Market Square)
- **Description:** Small bakery with sweet scent of danish and fine bread
- **Exits:** South (to Main Street)
- **NPCs:** The baker
- **Menu:**
  1. A danish pastry - 7 coins (Unlimited)
  2. A bread - 14 coins (Unlimited)
  3. A waybread - 70 coins (Unlimited)

## Earning Gold (Newbie Guide)

### Current situation
- **Level 1** Apprentice of Magic with **0 gold**
- **Movement** depletes when moving; regain by `sleep` or `rest`
- **Nighttime** makes outdoors pitch black — get a light source first
- **Hungry/Thirsty** — need food/drink

### How to earn gold
1. **Sleep** at the Temple Square to regain movement (`sleep`, then `wake`)
2. **Get a light source** — check the Magic Shop (north of Main Street west end) or the General Store for a lantern/torch
3. **Kill mobs** for gold coins — best areas for level 1:
   - **Newbie Farm** (levels 1-10) — accessible from Midgaard, easiest for newbies
   - **Plains outside West Gate** — kill animals (need light + weapon)
4. **Loot corpses** — `get all corpse` after kills to collect coins and items
5. **Sell unwanted loot** at shops with `sell <item>`
6. **Quests** — use `quest list` to see available quests, `quest join <num>` to start one
7. **Donation Room** — check the alcove east of The Temple of Midgaard for free starter gear
